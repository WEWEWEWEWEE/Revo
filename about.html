About Us

Established in the early 1990s, our hospital has grown from a small clinic in Colombo to a leading healthcare institution in Sri Lanka. With a vision to provide world-class medical care, we began in Bambalapitiya, quickly becoming known for our commitment to quality and patient-centered care. Over the years, we expanded our services to meet the diverse healthcare needs of our community, launching specialized departments such as cardiology, oncology, and neurology in the early 2000s.In 2010, we opened our specialized treatment branch in Kandy, further solidifying our reputation for medical excellence. Today, with multiple branches and over 200 dedicated professionals, we offer a comprehensive range of services, from routine check-ups to complex surgeries. Our commitment to innovation, research, and continuous improvement drives us to adopt the latest medical advancements and provide exceptional care. As we look to the future, we remain dedicated to expanding our services and enhancing the health and well-being of the communities we serve, building on our legacy as one of Sri Lankaâ€™s most trusted medical institutions.

Our Branches

Colombo

Located in the heart of Colombo, our main branch serves as a central hub for comprehensive healthcare services. Equipped with state-of-the-art medical technology and staffed by a team of experienced professionals, this facility offers a wide range of medical treatments and specialized care. Conveniently accessible, our Bambalapitiya branch is dedicated to providing exceptional patient care, ensuring the well-being of the community.

Kandy

Our Kandy branch focuses on providing specialized treatments across various medical fields to address complex and unique healthcare needs. Located in the beautiful city of Kandy, this facility is equipped with advanced medical technology and staffed by a team of expert specialists in fields such as neurology, cardiology, oncology, and more. Dedicated to delivering personalized care and innovative treatment options, our Kandy branch is committed to enhancing patient health and well-being.

Galle

**Advanced Care Branch - Galle** Our Galle branch is a modern healthcare facility equipped with the latest medical technology and advanced equipment to provide a wide range of diagnostic and treatment services. Situated in the historic city of Galle, this branch combines cutting-edge medical facilities with a team of highly qualified professionals dedicated to delivering exceptional care. Focused on precision medicine and innovative treatments, our Galle branch is committed to enhancing patient outcomes through state-of-the-art care in a comfortable and welcoming environment.
